Harris Jayaraj

Biographical Information

Harris Jayaraj is an Indian film composer from Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He is one of the most popular music composers in the Tamil film industry and is known for his melodic compositions in movies like "Minnale", "Kaakha Kaakha", and "Vaaranam Aayiram".

Discography

Minnale (2001)
Kaakha Kaakha (2003)
Anniyan (2005)
Vaaranam Aayiram (2008)
Engeyum Kadhal (2011)
